WELCOME TO ASIATIC

Mr. Rajesh Agrawal with a wide experience of 30 years in the field is heading India's leading manufacturer and exporter of dyestuff, established in 1976. We are an ISO 9001:2008, ISO 14001 & ISO 18001(OHSAS) certified organization, with Export House status. Our major products are pre-registered under REACH with only representative TUV. We produce products with international specification for Azo Amines, PCP-TECP- Tricp, Heavy Metal & NEPO & OEPO.

AI has its plant set up in the industrial and chemical zone of Western India, Naroda, Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Fully equipped with the most modern and sophisticated machinery, state-of- the-art production technology like Reverse Osmosis System to reduce salts & low molecule impurities, Spray dryers and etc. We recycle the contaminated water through Effluent Treatment Plant with the help of Reverse Osmosis System, taking advantage of most modern Membrane Technology.

After 30 years of perspiring team work, AI achieves respectable position in a dyestuff Industry. Currently we are producing 2000 MT Per annum. AI is a one stop customer shop for Acid Dyes, Direct Dyes & Reactive Dyes applicable on Leather, Fur, Wool, Nylon & Textiles & Paper/Industries thereby saving considerable time, energy & money for the customer as we selected product range i.e. most usable in industry. We have satisfied customers to all over the world. We welcome our clients to share feedback after sales which help us to improve and achieve the mission of utmost customer satisfaction.
